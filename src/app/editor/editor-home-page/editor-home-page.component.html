Update Notes

In March, tooltips will be implemented. Until then, please continue to use the current views. This page serves as a preview, and we hope you find it to your liking. You will have the ability to dismiss, pin, edit, and configure the tooltips as desired. All updates will be made available here, allowing you to stay informed on our progress and objectives.

Translations

We are pleased to announce that translations to major languages will be available sooner than previously expected. Additionally, you will have the flexibility to set your preferred currency. Furthermore, you will have the ability to edit any of the texts to better fit your unique style and preferences, in case the provided translations do not meet your expectations.

Previous Bonushunt Details

As of March 1, 2023, you will have the ability to access both past bonus hunts and tournaments, dating back to September 1, 2021.

Loyalty API

We are also pleased to announce the launch of our Loyalty API. This will allow developers to access and manage loyalty program data, enabling integration with other systems and streamlining processes. The API will provide a robust set of features, including the ability to create and manage rewards, track points and transactions, and access real-time data on customer engagement
